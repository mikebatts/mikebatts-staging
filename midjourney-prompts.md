# Midjourney Prompt Set — Roman Greco-Futurism

For creating imagery matching the aesthetic of the Romulus case study videos and media.
No ASCII effects — those are applied post-production.

---

## Key Style Tokens

**Composition:** deep linear perspective, central vanishing point, extreme architectural recession
**Lighting:** backlighting (contre-jour), chiaroscuro, soft atmospheric haze, ambient bounce light
**Color:** desaturated cool blue-grey, warm amber/ochre accent, muted teal reflections, dark vignette borders
**Mood:** sublime melancholy, romantic ruin, timeless grandeur
**Style:** Piranesi capriccio, Hubert Robert, 18th century Italian veduta, cinematic depth
**Parameters:** `--ar 16:9 --v 6.0 --style raw --stylize 750` (landscape)
**Parameters:** `--ar 9:16 --v 6.0 --style raw --stylize 750` (portrait)

## Midjourney Best Practices (V6)

- **Natural language works best** — write full descriptive sentences, not keyword soup
- **Order matters** — most important subject first, style modifiers last
- **`--style raw`** — prevents Midjourney from over-stylizing; keeps the classical vision intact
- **`--stylize` range:** 650-800 for architecture/art (higher = more artistic interpretation, lower = more literal)
- **`--v 6.0`** — current best for photorealism and classical art
- **Avoid these words** (V6 overuses them): "dramatic lighting", "stunning", "breathtaking", "epic"
- **Instead use:** specific lighting terms like "contre-jour", "chiaroscuro", "diffused ambient"
- **Aspect ratios:** `--ar 16:9` for landscape hero sections, `--ar 9:16` for portrait insets
- **Reference artists:** Giovanni Battista Piranesi, Hubert Robert, Giovanni Paolo Pannini
