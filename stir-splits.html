Stir Splits
2021 ∙ Product designer
Context
Creators thrive in a collaborative world, joining forces on projects across music, video, writing and more. Still, juggling financials and splitting earnings fairly, while managing multiple income streams, is a headache waiting to happen.
Problem
The tools available simply aren't built for creators' unique needs. Sharing revenue remains an overly complex tangle of spreadsheets, manual payouts, and crossed fingers. When making a living depends on seamless collaborations, there should be a better way.
Solution
Stir is dedicated to helping creators earn together through intuitive revenue sharing tools. No more piecemeal solutions. With Stir, you can:
  • Create automated "Splits" to share revenue streams with collaborators
  • Customize payment splits for each collaborator's role
  • Track payments and transfer earnings ownership seamlessly
Details
Familiarity, composability and presence were guiding principles for the Stir experience. Contextual onboarding walks users through creating their first Split, marking transactions, and inviting collaborators before ever connecting payment details.
The Splits dashboard keeps revenue sharing transparent, letting creators know who's being paid out and who's earning their cut for the corresponding pay period. Contributor titles, roles, and ownership are also clear to get the whole picture of your collaboration.
Impact
The future of creation is collaborative, and Stir provides long-awaited tools tailored for these emerging needs. By designing intuitive payment splitting flows anchored in familiar patterns and design principles, Stir's 'Splits' feature is a step towards more efficient product suites for creators.
